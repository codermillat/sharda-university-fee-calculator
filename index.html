Scholarship Information

Sharda University offers scholarships for eligible students. Students with GPA 3.5-5.0 can get up to 50% scholarship, and those with GPA 3.0-3.4 can get 20% scholarship on eligible programs like B.Tech, BBA, MBA, and BCA.
